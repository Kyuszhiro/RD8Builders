About Us

RD8 BUILDERS CORPORATION , is a dynamic construction firm that began its journey as a sole proprietorship in 2017, a construction firm that embodies the dreams and aspirations of its founders, Rampel and Rovelyn. This husband-and-wife duo has traansformed their initial vision into a thriving enterprise that not only meets but exceeds the expectations of clients and stakeholders alike. With a shared passion for construction and a commitment to excellence, they established the firm with a clear mission: to provide top-tier construction services that consistently meet and exceed client expectations. As the company rapidly expanded, it transitioned into a corporation by 2020, allowing for a broader scope of services and programs.


Rampel, as the Chief Executive Officer and Head of Marketing, brings a wealth of experience and strategic insight to the company. His passion for construction and dedication to excellence has propelled RD8 Builders Corporation from a Category D to a prestigious Triple A construction firm. This remarkable evolution is a testament to the hard work, determination, and unwavering commitment of them both.


Rovelyn, serving as the Chief Financial Officer, plays a crucial role in the financial management and sustainability of the business. Her keen business acumen and attention to detail ensure that RD8 Builders Corporation not only maintains profitability but also invests in innovative practices and sustainable solutions. By adhering to a strict quality management system, RD8 Builders Corporation has achieved significant milestones, notably attaining ISO 9001:2015 compliance, which underscores its commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.


The firm began its operations with a modest categorization but, through relentless dedication and hard work, advanced to a Triple A construction company status by 2023 with ISO 9001:2015 in 2024. This transformation is a testament to RD8's unwavering focus on quality and a steadfast commitment to its core values. At RD8 Builders Corporation, the philosophy of quality is paramount. The firm focuses on adherence to all applicable requirements, ensuring that every project is executed with precision and professionalism.


The company prides itself on delivering quality work that is not only profitable but also completed within the agreed timelines.This dedication to excellence is complemented by a strong emphasis on personnel development, where the company invests in training and resources to enhance the competencies of its workforce. This commitment to team development ensures that the staff is well-equipped to meet the evolving demands of the construction industry. Innovation and continuous improvement form the backbone of RD8 Builders Corporation's operations.


The firm actively seeks out new methodologies and technologies to enhance its Quality Management System (QMS), striving for excellence at every stage of the construction process. By fostering a culture of innovation, RD8 not only meets the current needs of its clients but also anticipates future demands, positioning itself as a leader in the construction sector. With a strong foundation built on quality, compliance, and customer satisfaction, RD8 Builders Corporation looks forward to a future of continued growth and success in the ever-evolving landscape of construction.
